4-Bar Linkage Mechanism

Kinematics & Grashof Analysis

Design Principles

The four-bar linkage is the simplest movable closed-chain linkage. It consists of four bodies, called bars or links, connected in a loop by four joints. My focus was analyzing the Grashof condition to predict the behavior of the linkage (Crank-Rocker vs. Double-Rocker).
