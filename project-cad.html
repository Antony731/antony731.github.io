Design Philosophy

I specialize in creating robust, parametric models that capture true design intent. My workflow bridges the gap between conceptual 2D sketches and fully defined 3D solid bodies, ensuring models are inherently stable, easily editable, and fully optimized for real-world manufacturing.

Core Capabilities

  • Complex Assemblies: Managing large-scale multi-body assemblies with rigorous interference detection and dynamic mating conditions.
  • Sheet Metal Design: Developing accurate flat patterns, calculating bend allowances, and preparing cuts for direct fabrication.
  • Production Drafting: Generating highly detailed, manufacturing-ready 2D drawings incorporating strict GD&T standards (ISO 1101) and comprehensive BOMs.
