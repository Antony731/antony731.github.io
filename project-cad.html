Design Philosophy

I specialize in creating robust parametric models that are easy to edit and manufacture. My workflow involves moving from rough 2D sketches to complex 3D surface and solid bodies, ensuring all design intent is captured in the feature tree.

Capabilities

  • Complex Assemblies: Managing large assemblies with mating conditions and interference detection.
  • Sheet Metal Design: Creating flat patterns, bends, and cuts for fabrication.
  • Drafting: Generating production-ready 2D drawings with proper GD&T and BOMs.
  • Rendering: Using tools like KeyShot or SolidWorks Visualize to create photorealistic images for presentations.
